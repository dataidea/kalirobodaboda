Frequently Asked Questions

How can I apply for membership in Kaliro Boda Association?

To apply for membership, please visit our office located at Local District, Kamuli Rd, Kaliro. You can also reach out to us via email at kaliroboda@gmail.com or call us at +25671754118 for more information.

What services are offered by Kaliro Boda Association?

We offer a range of services for boda boda riders, including loans for motorcycle financing, transportation services for boda boda users, and consultation services for riders to improve their businesses. We also plan to introduce other services in the future.

How can I contact Kaliro Boda Association for more information?

You can reach out to us via email at kaliroboda@gmail.com or call us at +25671754118. You can also visit our office at Local District, Kamuli Rd, Kaliro. Our team will be happy to assist you with any inquiries you may have.

How does the transportation service work for boda boda users?

The transportation service provides boda boda users with a convenient and affordable way to get around the city. Simply book a ride with our registered riders through our app or by calling us. Our riders will pick you up and take you to your destination quickly and safely.
